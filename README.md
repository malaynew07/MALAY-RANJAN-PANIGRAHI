## 💡 What I bring from production support

Three years of troubleshooting production systems has taught me something DevOps bootcamps can't: **how systems actually fail in the real world.**

A recent example — a gross-weight submission from our integration platform to SAP started returning 400 errors mid-workflow. Tracing it through logs, the root cause wasn't in our platform at all: SAP was sending incorrect material weight during delivery-order allocation, creating a gross/tare mismatch downstream. Resolution needed coordinated action across three teams — SAP (fix the source data), the weighbridge integration team (revert gross for re-assignment), and our platform (re-trigger). Resolved same day.

That's the kind of multi-system thinking I'm bringing into SRE work: reading logs as evidence, tracing blast radius across service boundaries, and owning the fix end-to-end.
